Jake & Blue

Jake & Blue’s Raw Dog Food was founded eight years ago. Our Combo Blend consists of a Beef, Goat and Lamb mix, with a percentage of muscle meat, organs, fat, bone meal, and ground trachea. These come in 5 pound tubes, which I cut into approximately 5 to 7 oz. pucks. Also available is our beef blend with muscle meat, organs, ground trachea and fat. These come in 1 1/2 LB. tubes which I also cut into 6 to 7 oz. pucks. No Fruits, Veggies or Additives. Our product is designed for Carnivores, which dog’s and cat’s digestive system are designed for.
